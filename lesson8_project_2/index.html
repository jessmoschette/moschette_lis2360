East Atlanta Love Letter by 6LACK

This is 6LACK's second and newest album, which was released on September 14, 2018. The cover to this album is a picture of him with his daughter, Syx Rose Valentine. It features the artists Khalid, Offset, J. Cole, and Future. The songs on this album are not only great sounding, but the lyrics are extremely emotional. 6LACK gets very deep and shares a lot of emotion within this amazing piece of work.

Take Care by Drake

This album came out on November 15, 2011 and is his second released studio album. Drake has a total of 5 studio albums, 6 mixtapes, and 131 singles. Take Care is a very emotional album that explores his experiences with fame, romance, and wealth. He also touches on relationships with friends and family. The artists featured on this album are The Weeknd, Rihanna, Kendrick Lamar, Birdman, Nicki Minaj, Rick Ross, Lil Wayne, and Andre 3000.

DS2 by Future

DS2, which stands for Dirty Sprite 2, is Future's third album and was released on July 17, 2015. It is the sequel to his mixtape, called Dirty Sprite. The only feature on this album is Drake, but Metro Boomin, Southside, and Zaytoven all worked with producing it. This album goes under the "trap" genre, considering all of the songs on it are very hype.
